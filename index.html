About Me

I am Kinley Yangchen, and I am from Trashigang. I like many things, like reading books, watching movies, and playing football and basketball. I also like listening to music and talking to people.

My time at The Royal Academy was a journey of slow and good growth. At first, I was not sure what to do in the future. But I always liked biology, so now I want to study MBBS after grade 12. I think this job will help me and other people. My growth is because of people who helped me and because I worked hard when things were difficult.
